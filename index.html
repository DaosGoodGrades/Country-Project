France, one of the oldest nations on Earth, located in Europe, is a country with a rich history, culture and cuisine. Today, it is also known as the country of love, making it the most visited country in the entire world.

The national flag of France (drapeau national de la France) is a tricolour featuring three vertical bands coloured blue (hoist side), white, and red. The design was adopted after the French Revolution, whose revolutionaries were influenced by the horizontally striped red-white-blue flag of the Netherlands.
